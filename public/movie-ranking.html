The Best Movies According to James!

These are my all time favourite movies. Or films. Depending on what your flavour is.

1. Back to the Future

Funny, clever, thrilling, heavy. Quite frankly the most perfect film.

2. Spirited Away

Very charming and very sweet. Ghibli at its very best. (Yes, I am aware that it is everyone's favourite Ghibli film but this is my house and it is my rules.)

3. Shaun of the Dead

How can one film have so many quotable lines? Only Edgar Wright can.
